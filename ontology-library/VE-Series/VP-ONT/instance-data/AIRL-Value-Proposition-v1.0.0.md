## 3. Problems We Solve

### 3.1 Strategic Level (CTO/CIO)

**Unstructured AI Adoption at Enterprise Scale**

CTOs and CIOs struggle with AI adoption without structured readiness assessment when scaling from pilots to enterprise-wide deployment, resulting in:
- 70-85% AI project failure rates
- $5M-$50M wasted investment per failed initiative
- Loss of competitive advantage to AI-native competitors

**Severity:** Critical | **Frequency:** Constant | **Growing:** Yes

**Pain Points:**
1. **High AI Project Failure Rate** -- 70-85% of enterprise AI projects fail pilot-to-production. Each failed initiative costs $5M-$50M in direct costs plus 12-24 months delay. *(Economic: $15M impact)*
2. **No Quantified Readiness Baseline** -- Cannot quantify current AI readiness state, making it impossible to set realistic targets, measure progress, or justify investment to the board. *(Functional: 200 hours wasted)*

### 3.2 Tactical Level (Programme Director)

**Cross-Domain AI Maturity Blindspots**

AI Programme Directors lack cross-domain visibility across strategy, people, process, technology, and governance dimensions, resulting in:
- Siloed assessments
- Inconsistent maturity scoring
- Inability to identify critical dependency gaps

**Severity:** Major | **Frequency:** Frequent | **Growing:** Yes

**Pain Points:**
3. **Siloed Maturity Assessment** -- Assessments conducted in technology silos without visibility into organisational, process, governance, and people dimensions. 60% of failure factors are missed. *(Functional: 60% gap)*
4. **Consultant Dependency** -- Organisations dependent on expensive external consultants ($2K-$5K/day). Assessment costs $200K-$500K per engagement; knowledge leaves when consultants leave. *(Economic: $350K impact)*

### 3.3 Operational Level (AI/Data Manager)

**Manual Assessment Overhead**

AI/Data Managers rely on spreadsheets and manual processes for readiness assessment, producing inconsistent and non-repeatable results.

**Severity:** Major | **Frequency:** Frequent

**Pain Points:**
5. **Spreadsheet Assessment Overhead** -- Manual assessments consume 40+ hours per cycle, produce inconsistent results across assessors, and cannot be meaningfully compared over time. *(Economic: 40 hours/cycle)*

### Problem Roll-Up Hierarchy

  +-- Tactical: Cross-Domain Blindspots (Major)
        +-- Operational: Manual Assessment Overhead (Major)
