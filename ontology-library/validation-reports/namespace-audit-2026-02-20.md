## Spaced-Phrase Relationship Naming (Legacy)

## Recommendations

1. **Priority 1 — `ns:` collision resolution:** CTX-ONT, RMF-IS27005-ONT, and ANTIQUES-ONT all share the `ns:` prefix. Upgrade to canonical prefixes (`ctx:`, `rmf:`, `antq:`) in next major version.

2. **Priority 2 — Legacy prefix migration:** ORG-ONT (`pf:` → `org:`), KPI-ONT (`pfc:` → `kpi:`), RRR-ONT (`pf:`/`rbac:` → `rrr:`), ORG-MAT-ONT (`mat:` → `org-mat:`).

3. **Priority 3 — Relationship naming migration:** Hub ontologies (GRC-FW-ONT, VSOM-ONT, EMC-ONT) should migrate spaced-phrase relationships to camelCase first, as they are referenced most heavily.
